# kemenyapprox

Approximates the Kemeny Ranking on sushi

1 - ebi (shrimp) 
2 - anago (sea eel) 
3 - maguro (tuna) 
4 - ika (squid) 
5 - uni (sea urchin) 
6 - sake (salmon roe) 
7 - tamago (egg) 
8 - toro (fatty tuna) 
9 - tekka-maki (tuna roll) 
10 - kappa-maki (cucumber roll)
